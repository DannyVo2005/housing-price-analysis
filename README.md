# Housing Price Trends Analysis

## Overview
Analyzing housing price trends across U.S. metro areas using Zillow dataset from 2000–2026 with Power BI. The price of houses plays a major factor in affordability, cost of living, and the economy as a whole. The price of houses has been subject to various dynamics in the past few years due to various reasons such as inflation, interest rates, and supply and demand.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Wrangling
- Data Visualization

## Key Features
- Time-series housing analysis
- Housing price growth comparison
- Interactive slicer allowing for filtering by state and city
- Geographic housing visualization

## Key Insights
- Housing prices generally increased over time
- Significant regional differences exist
- Housing prices declined around 2008–2012 before recovering from financial crisis
- Some regions experienced a faster growth rate than others
- Affordability varies depending on region
